About Me

As a lifelong learner, I have consistently pushed myself to explore and discover new things. This drive has taken me to some amazing places such as working at an elephant sanctuary in Thailand, teaching English in France and Chile, and even working as a raptor handler. This drive brought me into a coding bootcamp through Northwestern University followed by another such program with Revature.

Since then, I have been working with two massive international companies, Cognizant and Dun & Bradstreet. In addition to that, I have been working as the sole developer at a startup called HowlingWolfe Canoe and Kayak.

I am a fullstack developer with a passion for the front end. Most of my experience is with Angular and Java, however I love learning new technologies and pick things up pretty quickly.
